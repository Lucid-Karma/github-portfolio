Overview:
Chibi Burger Cafe is a cozy, chaotic burger-making game where players craft custom burgers in a vibrant, character-driven kitchen. Players can build burgers freely ‚Äî stacking ingredients in any order, skipping prep, or mastering the perfect grill. The game celebrates creativity and humor while testing players‚Äô timing and organization.

Key Features:

  • Dynamic Burger Creation: Freely stack ingredients ‚Äî neat or wild ‚Äî to craft your perfect burger. The game evaluates creativity, completeness, and quality in real-time.
  • Chef Feedback System: A grumpy but lovable head chef watches your every move and reacts dynamically, adding pressure and personality.

  • Cosmetic Customization: Players can personalize their character's look, adding flavor to the experience.

Technical Highlights:

  • Built on robust software architecture using key design patterns:
    • Finite State Machine: Manages game states, character behavior, and customer flow efficiently.
    • Factory Pattern: Dynamically creates interactable objects (ingredients, tools) based on gameplay context.
    • Observer Pattern: Ensures decoupled, event-driven communication between UI, customer ratings, and chef behavior.
    • Singleton Pattern: Centralized management of game systems like UI, level flow, and scoring.
    • Object Pooling: Optimizes performance by reusing frequently spawned objects like ingredients and effects.
